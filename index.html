Driving Digital Strategy

A chapter-by-chapter podcast guide

Chapter 1 – Rethinking Business Scope

"In a digital world, companies must rethink the scope of their business—not just what they do, but what problems they solve for customers."

Chapter 2 – Reinventing Business Models

"Digitization allows you to decouple value creation from value capture."

Chapter 3 – Platform Strategy

"In platform businesses, value is created not by controlling resources, but by orchestrating interactions."

Chapter 4 – Open Innovation

"You can’t innovate effectively in isolation. The best ideas may come from outside your walls."

Chapter 5 – Digital Operations

"Digital transformation is not about automation—it’s about reimagining how work gets done."

Chapter 6 – Omnichannel Strategy

"Customers don’t think in channels. They think in experiences."

Chapter 7 – Customer Acquisition

"The traditional sales funnel is dead. Today, customer journeys are nonlinear, personalized, and dynamic."

Chapter 8 – Customer Engagement

"Engagement is no longer about pushing messages—it’s about creating meaningful, ongoing interactions."

Chapter 9 – Marketing Measurement

"You can’t manage what you can’t measure—but beware of measuring the wrong things."

Chapter 10 – Managing the Transition

"Culture eats strategy for breakfast, especially during digital transformation."

Chapter 11 – Landing Dock

"Even the best ideas need a place to land—or they’ll crash before they scale."

Chapter 12 – Talent and Leadership

"You can buy technology, but you can’t buy transformation. That requires people."
